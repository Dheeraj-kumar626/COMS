Short Description
This a Simple web App where one can issue,reissue,contribute books to library

About
Its Main Objective is to manage library books Online .The Users can perform renewal,issuing of books and 
can also contribute the books to the library by the approval of Admin.
They can also know the Fine they have to pay to the library 

Driving Force/BackGround
The Disgust caused by standing in long queues for returning and Issuing books
